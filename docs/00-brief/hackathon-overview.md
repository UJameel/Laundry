## Hackathon Profile

**Type:** Domain-specific (AI for Finance) with sponsor tool integration
**Competitive intensity:** Medium-High — strong judges (Intuit, Salesforce, Google, SBI), press release prize, SF tech crowd
**Key constraints:**
- Duration: ~5.5 hours of actual build time
- Demo: 3-minute video submission + likely live demo
- Team: 3-5 recommended
- Submission: Video demo + live presentation

**Strategic implications:**
- **Real-world impact is king (25pts).** Don't build a toy — pick a real pain point in finance and solve it convincingly.
- **Product execution & demo quality tie with impact (25pts).** With only 3 minutes, the demo MUST be polished. Allocate 45-60 min for demo prep.
- **Sponsor tool usage is implicitly weighted.** 4 sponsors with credits = they want to see their tools used. Using 2-3 sponsors well signals you engaged with the ecosystem.
- **5.5 hours is brutal.** Scope must be razor-tight. One feature done well beats three half-baked features.
- **Judge backgrounds matter.** Product manager from Intuit + GTM from Google = they'll value product thinking and market fit, not just technical wizardry.
- **Common mistake:** Building too much, demoing poorly. The demo IS the product at a hackathon.
- **Finance domain:** Judges will know finance. Don't fake domain expertise — pick a sub-vertical you understand or can quickly validate.
